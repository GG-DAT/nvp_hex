Discover the Dynamics of Utrecht, Netherlands

Insights into Walking Speeds

Explore Utrecht’s lively rhythm with this map, displaying average walking speeds collected from participant smartphones over the course of a year. More than 2,238 individuals and 25,104 journeys are reflected in this unique dataset.

The data is meticulously screened to ensure accuracy, focusing solely on walking speeds. It's part of the esteemed Nederland’s Verplaatsing Panel (NVP), continuously tracking about 10,000 Dutch residents globally since 2019.
